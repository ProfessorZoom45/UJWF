B-Wilder just changed the board.

Full recap to current

Monday Night Jabs Week 6 changed the record book: B-Wilder pinned I_Do_Dis_314 to become the new Unprovoked Heavyweight Champion. Brass_Monkey_NYC beat Gamechanger97, SeanBz206 stopped Jonathansb316, Saman747 got revenge on Major_Smoove, and G2_daJuice24 pinned Freexsmoke91.

Friday Night Fades Week 5 shook up the contender boards too: That951boi beat Califooya, Team Kentucky survived the four-team tornado tag, The Legends beat Tha Product, Ramoja3 won the battle royal, and B-Wilder pinned M0ney_T510 before taking the top title on MNJ.
